Developing UCLA's future consultants, entrepreneurs, and business leaders

Our Mission

UConsulting is the premier undergraduate student consulting organization at UCLA. Founded with the belief that the consulting industry should be accessible to all backgrounds, UConsulting is dedicated to providing our members the resources and opportunities to find professional success in consulting.

Our Services

For Students

  • Learn the skills necessary to excel as a management consultant through weekly meetings and workshops.

  • Along with exclusive tours and networking sessions with top consulting firms and guest speakers, we also pride ourselves in our community with regular socials and retreats.

  • Through direct exposure to real business problems through our quarterly project cycles, members can hone their analytical skills.
